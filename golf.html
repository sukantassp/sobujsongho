GOLF

The 18 hole, 70 par 100-acre championship golf course is designed by internationally reputed golf architects. The all season course is rich in challenge and natural appeal. It caters to different skill levels and provides fresh insights on every visit. Our dedicated staff, modern equipment and the underground irrigation system maintain the course in top condition through the year. The golf course is an oasis of green nested in the heart of urban Kolkata. A floodlit driving range and illuminated putting and chipping green give you an opportunity to hone your skills even after the sun has gone down. Our practice range, putting green and private lessons provide you with a one-of-a-kind golf experience.

Coaches

Set amongst the garden of Tollygunge Club are one outdoor swimming pool and one indoor swimming pool featuring a diving board, recreational pool and an additional kid’s pool! The indoor pool is heated in winter.

Pool Timings
  • 5:30 am to 8.30 pm daily for both Ladies and Gentlemen.
  • Monday Outdoor Pool is Closed & Indoor Pool is open till 2 pm
Charges for Guests
  • Guests are welcome with Club Members only.
  • Rs 150.00 for week days.
  • Rs 250.00 for weekends & holidays.
Guest Timings
  • Same as members.

Achievers

The Club has 2 billiards tables and one pool table. Billiards, snooker and eight-ball and nine-ball pool are popular among members. Weekday timings are from 2.00 pm to 9:00 pm and members can enjoy an extended game on weekends and holidays from 11:00 am to 9:00 pm. A monthly tournament adds excitement to the fixtures. The Club boasts of members who have won the national and world championships.

Billiards Practice Timings
  • Weekdays – 2:00 pm to 9:00 pm
  • Saturday / Sunday & Holidays 11:00 am to 9:00 pm (Closed on Monday)
