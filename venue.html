Far Pavilion

Facilities:
  • Upper Lawn Terrace: 3000 sqft, Lower Lawn (with permission)
  • Large & Small Room: 1501 sqft Air conditioned, Customised Designer Buffet Canopies, Open-air Stage, Overlooking the Golf Course
Seating:
  • Large & Small Hall: Cluster Style - up to 50
  • Buffet Area: Adjacent to Inbuilt Bar Counter
  • Hall Only: Up to 100 Hall & Upper Lawn: Up to 200
  • Hall with Upper & Lower Lawn: Up to 400

Main Lawn

  • Facilities: Half Lawn at 14000 sq ft and Full Lawn at 28000 sq ft, with a 900 sq ft attached bar counter
  • Seating: Theatre Style: Up to 300 Cluster Style: Up to 200. Ideal for outdoor events (no music allowed), offering a perfect blend of nature and elegance.

Pantiles

Ideal for relaxed gatherings, this charming space offers coffee and treats from Flurys and drinks from nearby bars. In summer, guests can cool down under large floor fans, while in winter, they can snuggle up around white wicker chairs for a cosy atmosphere. Pantiles is occasionally lit up by the Live at Pantiles concerts, making it an excellent choice for concerts and special events.

Patio 1St Floor Terrace

  • Open Terrace: 3600 sqft
  • Seating: Cluster Style for up to 40. Perfect for club catered events, enjoy the open-air ambience and stunning views.

Seating:
  • U-Style: Up to 35
  • Theatre Style: Up to 70
  • Boardroom: Up to 40
  • Cluster Style: Up to 48
  • Standing Buffet: Up to 75 pax
