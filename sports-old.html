Sports

The 18 hole, 70 par 100-acre championship golf course is designed by internationally reputed golf architects. The all season course is rich in challenge and natural appeal. It caters to different skill levels and provides fresh insights on every visit. Our dedicated staff, modern equipment and the underground irrigation system maintain the course in top condition through the year. The golf course is an oasis of green nested in the heart of urban Kolkata. A floodlit driving range and illuminated putting and chipping green give you an opportunity to hone your skills even after the sun has gone down. Our practice range, putting green and private lessons provide you with a one-of-a-kind golf experience.

Gym

“Adrenalize” the gym at the Tolly is a haven for those who wish to keep fit. The warm and friendly environment adds to the attraction of this gym. Contemporary equipment spread over a large area on two levels is supported by trained staff who are there to assist members in planning their fitness routine.

Treadmills, rowing machines, stair walkers, exercise cycles and cross trainers etc. are among some of the machines that are available. In addition to machines and weights, Yoga, Zumba and classes by renowned fitness experts are conducted for those desiring a different kind of workout.

The rates for members using the facilities are among the lowest in the country and this also adds to the popularity of “Adrenalize”.

Timings :

  • 6 AM – 2 PM (Monday)

Billiads

The Club has 2 billiards tables and one pool table. Billiards, snooker and eight-ball and nine-ball pool are popular among members. Weekday timings are from 2.00 pm to 9:00 pm and members can enjoy an extended game on weekends and holidays from 11:00 am to 9:00 pm. A monthly tournament adds excitement to the fixtures. The Club boasts of members who have won the national and world championships.

Billiards Practice Timings
  • Weekdays – 2:00 pm to 9:00 pm
  • Saturday / Sunday & Holidays 11:00 am to 9:00 pm (Closed on Monday)

Darts

Darts dates back to more than 700 years, originally played by the English and French military. Today, Darts is not only well known as a traditional pub sport but a professional and elite shooting sport as well.

Darts can be played by anyone, it is easy to set up, simple to learn, has a low cost to entry and is fun to watch, all adding to its worldwide popularity. As a result, Darts is now not only a pub sport but also played professionally in arenas, packed with thousands of spectators and hundreds of thousands more watching from home on live television. Apart from challenging your brain, your hand-eye coordination, the sport also exercises the cerebrum. Tolly has 4 boards with one of them being in the “Johny Walker bar”. The Club boasts of 40 regular darters as members, male and female, who have won various inter-club events.

Bridge

There are two well-appointed bridge rooms in the ground floor of the main club building. Currently there are more than 50 active bridge players and most of them participate in regular club tournaments. These tournaments are held on the second and fourth Saturday of each month for members only. And in addition, on the third Sunday of each month there is an invitational tournament where members can play with a partner from any of the other social clubs in Kolkata.

Also, occasionally, theme tournaments are held, sometimes only for members and at times members of other clubs are included. Once a year, the inter-club satellite bridge tournament is held where other social clubs are invited to play both as pairs and teams. Quite a few of Tolly’s bridge players have carved a name for themselves.

Bridge Practice Timings
  • 11.00 am to 7.00 pm (Closed on Monday)
