Our Club Tour

  • Heritage

    The Tollygunge Club in Kolkata is India’s premier country club, ranked among the top 20 clubs globally. Spanning 100 acres, it features a clubhouse over 220 years old and is located just 9 km from central Kolkata. Founded in 1895 for British merchants and bankers, Tollygunge Club started as an equestrian facility and has evolved into a versatile club with unique flora and fauna. It offers golf, tennis, squash, swimming, and equestrian sports, bridge room, salon, and ayurvedic center.
