Booking Rules

Terms & Condition
  • 100% Payment of the Venue charge has to be paid in advance by the Member at the time of the booking.
  • Members are request to deposit the estimated cost of the event for the Food & Beverages and the other miscellaneous charges 7 days prior to the event against Club’s Proforma Invoice. Payment will be made only by Cheque / electronic mode.
  • The final adjustments will be made after the event, against the final bill.
  • If a Member wishes to use any of the empanelled caterers of the Club, cover charges of Rs.100/- per plate + service tax (as applicable) will be levied.
  • The Members will be charged a minimum, based on the number of guests guaranteed. If the number of guests increases, charges will be levied as applicable.
  • Cancellation charges for Venues are as follows
    • 100 % refund if cancelled above 30 days from the date of the function;
    • 75% refund for 21-29 days;
    • 50% refund for 15-20 days;
    • 25% refund for 2-14 days;
    • No refund for less than 2 days.
  • Cancellation of less than 48 hours on Food orders will attract 100% charges.
  • If, due to unforeseen circumstances, the Club cancels the booking, members will be given an advance notice to the extent possible and 100% refund will be given or an alternative venue will be offered, subject to availability
  • Bar services will shut at 10:30 p.m. as per the Club Rules.
  • Except for the AC Auditorium; no music for private events is permitted.
  • The Club does not permit any religious services / rituals in its premises.
  • All private parties and functions in the Club must close by 11 p.m.
